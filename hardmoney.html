Programs

Hard Money & Bridge Programs

Short-term, asset-based financing for real estate investors. We underwrite on the deal — the property, your plan, and the numbers — not just your tax return.

F+F

Fix & Flip

Finance up to 90% of purchase price plus 100% of rehab costs based on After Repair Value (ARV). Fast closes — often 5–10 business days. Perfect for single-family, multifamily, and small commercial flips.

  • Up to 90% LTC
  • 100% rehab
  • Based on ARV
  • 5–10 day close
  • SFR to small commercial
GU

Ground-Up Construction

New construction financing for residential and small commercial projects. Draw-based funding released as work is completed. Ideal for experienced builders and developers with a clear scope and timeline.

  • Residential & commercial
  • Draw schedule
  • Infill & subdivision
  • 12–24 month terms
BR

BRRRR & Rental Bridge

Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. We fund the acquisition and rehab, you stabilize the property, then we help you refinance into a long-term DSCR loan. The full cycle in one lender relationship.

  • Buy + rehab bridge
  • Stabilize & refi
  • DSCR exit
  • Portfolio friendly
BRG

Bridge Loans

Short-term bridge financing for time-sensitive acquisitions, 1031 exchange gaps, estate sales, and commercial properties in transition. Close fast while long-term financing is arranged.

  • 6–24 month terms
  • Fast close
  • 1031 bridge
  • Estate & distressed
LND

Land & Lot Loans

Acquisition financing for entitled and raw land, infill lots, and development-ready parcels. Difficult to finance conventionally — we have lenders who specialize in land and lot lending.

  • Entitled land
  • Raw land
  • Infill lots
  • Development parcels
CB

Commercial Bridge

Short-term financing for commercial assets in transition — lease-up, repositioning, renovation, or acquisition with value-add upside. Close on the asset now, stabilize, then refinance into permanent financing.

  • Lease-up
  • Repositioning
  • Value-add
  • 12–36 month terms

What We Look At

Hard money lending is asset-based. We care about the property, your plan, the numbers, and your experience. Credit score matters less here — the deal and the ARV matter most.

First-time investors are welcome. We work with new and experienced flippers alike.

Documents Typically Needed

  • Purchase agreement or property details
  • Scope of work with contractor bids
  • Comparable sales (comps) supporting ARV
  • Photos of the property (interior + exterior)
  • Government-issued ID
  • Entity documents (if purchasing in LLC)
  • Track record / list of past projects (if experienced)
  • Exit strategy documentation
  • Last 3 months bank statements (shows liquidity)
